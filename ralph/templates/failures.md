## How to Use

When Ralph encounters repeated issues (same error 2+ times), log it here:

```markdown
## [Issue Name]
- **First seen**: Iteration N
- **Times occurred**: X
- **Description**: What went wrong
- **Root cause**: Why it happened
- **Solution**: How it was fixed
- **Promote to guardrail?**: Yes/No
```
